Everything you need to know about joining Unrestricted RP

How do I join the server? +

Simply click the "Connect" button on our homepage or use the server code "jyrm3l" in FiveM. Make sure you have FiveM installed and join our Discord for whitelist information if required.

Do I need to be whitelisted? +

Check our Discord server for current whitelist requirements. Some roles may require applications, but general access is typically open to all players who follow our rules.

What are the server requirements? +

You need a legal copy of GTA V, FiveM installed, a working microphone for voice chat, and a stable internet connection. We recommend at least 8GB RAM for optimal performance.

Can I play as a criminal? +

Absolutely! We support both legal and criminal roleplay. You can run businesses, join gangs, participate in heists, or pursue a life of crime - just remember to roleplay realistically and follow server rules.

How do I apply for police or EMS? +

Join our Discord server and check the applications channel. You will need to fill out an application form and may need to attend an interview. Make sure you meet the minimum playtime requirements.

Is there an age requirement? +

Yes, players must be at least 16 years old to join our server. This ensures mature roleplay and helps maintain a respectful community environment.
