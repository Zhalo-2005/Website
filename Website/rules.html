2. Community Conduct

  • 2.0 Respect and Behaviour: Respect all members in Discord and in the server. We have a zero tolerance policy for bullying, harassment, or stalking.
  • 2.1 Discrimination: No form of discrimination will be tolerated.
  • 2.2 Personal Attacks / Bullying: No harmful, offensive, or derogatory behaviour towards another member‚Äôs real-life identity.
  • 2.3 Discord Status/Name: No profanity or offensive names.
  • 2.4 Scripts, Hacking & Exploits: No external scripts, hacks, or exploiting bugs. Report bugs instead of abusing them, you may even be rewarded.
  • 2.5 Advertising:
    • 2.5.1 Links ‚Äì No links to other communities.
    • 2.5.2 Other ‚Äì No sharing content promoting other servers.
    • 2.5.3 Verbal/IC ‚Äì No discussing or advertising other servers while in city.
  • 2.6 Support Structure: Follow the support chain (Discord staff ‚Üí Admin ‚Üí Community Manager ‚Üí Owners). Do not DM staff directly.
  • 2.7 Staff Impersonation: Do not impersonate staff members in Discord or the server.
  • 2.8 Trading and Selling Items: No real-money trades. All purchases must go through the official store.
  • 2.9 Whitelisted Job Items: Do not carry, rob, or use whitelisted job items without prior staff-approved RP.

3. Roleplay Rules

  • 3.0 Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM): Using vehicles as weapons is not allowed.
  • 3.1 Random Deathmatch (RDM): Attacking players without proper roleplay is not allowed.
    • 3.1.1 Taking a life is a serious thing, there must be a legitimate reason for it.
    • 3.1.2 We expect a minimum of 2 minutes of interaction prior to killing someone.
  • 3.2 Baiting: Deliberately provoking police/gangs to force conflict is not allowed.
  • 3.3 Spam: No spamming in chats (/me, Twitter, etc.).
  • 3.4 Powergaming:
    • 3.4.1 Do not force quit/log out of the game during a scene.
    • 3.4.2 Do not force outcomes or use unrealistic actions/items to gain advantage.
    • 3.4.3 Do not use in-game mechanics or exploits to your advantage (e.g., spam punching, animation abuse, placing items in the ‚ÄúAbyssal bins‚Äù to avoid arrest, etc.).
    • 3.4.4 Do not use ‚ÄúBlack zones‚Äù / loading screens to your advantage ‚Äì e.g., if someone is coming off an elevator, do not instantly shoot them.
    • 3.4.5 Icy doesn't like pizza.
  • 3.5 Separating In/Out of Character: Keep IC and OOC issues separate.
  • 3.6 Metagaming: Using OOC info IC is prohibited.
    • 3.6.1 Voice ID ‚Äì Cannot ID players by voice/session IDs.
    • 3.6.2 In-Game Birdie ‚Äì Birdie is IC only.
    • 3.6.3 Character Separation ‚Äì Characters must remain independent, your souls don't know what your other souls have seen or been up to, they do not speak while you are not around.
    • 3.6.4 Stream sniping ‚Äì You must not be watching streams of people who are in the server whilst you, yourself, are also in the server.
  • 3.7 Realistic Driving: Drive realistically. RP accidents properly. (No GTA driving, e.g., jumps, on-road cars going unrealistically off-road or up mountain sides, driving with no tyres, etc.)
    • 3.7.1 Suicide Ramming ‚Äì Not allowed.
    • 3.7.2 PD Car Swap ‚Äì Police cannot swap cars mid-pursuit. They must repair their vehicle if possible before returning to the pursuit.
  • 3.8 New Life Rule (NLR) / Scene Continuation: On respawn, your character forgets prior events.
    • 3.8.1 You will forget either the entire scene OR 30 minutes (whichever is longer).
    • 3.8.2 If you are part of a scene, the scene is still ongoing for 20 minutes AFTER the last interaction (e.g., if you stab someone and run away, they have every right to stab you back for up to 20 mins without needing to explain why).
  • 3.9 Names & Plates: Characters must have realistic names. Plates must be appropriate.

4. Advanced Roleplay Rules

  • 4.0 Perma Deaths: Only possible with mutual agreement and staff being notified.
  • 4.1 Hostages: Hostages must be genuine (you cannot use a friend as a hostage, this is power gaming). High-quality RP is expected.
  • 4.2 Emergency Services Rules:
    • 4.2.1 Switching Jobs ‚Äì 7 days cooldown between whitelisted jobs.
    • 4.2.2 Vehicles ‚Äì BLHS vehicles cannot be stolen. PD cars can only be taken as a last resort.
  • 4.3 Roleplay Injuries: You must RP your injuries until respawn.
  • 4.4 Fail RP: No unrealistic RP.
    • 4.4.1 Must have items in your inventory to use them (e.g., zip ties, bags, gloves).
    • 4.4.2 Must physically take comms (phones/radios).
  • 4.5 Ninja Looting / Storing: No pocket wiping, unrealistic storing, or removing all items without justification.
  • 4.6 Killing and Robbing: You may rob and kill as long as there is roleplay to back this up.
  • 4.7 Banned Roleplay Topics:
    • No Terrorism RP.
    • No Suicide RP.
    • No Pregnancy RP.
    • No ERP.
    • No Mental Health RP (unless approved).
  • 4.8 Sexualising Members: No sexualising other members.
  • 4.9 Fail to Fear: Value your life realistically.
    • 4.9.1 Fear RP ‚Äì If held at gunpoint, fear for your life.
    • 4.9.2 Taser ‚Äì If tased, you must RP the effects 3 times and you're out (unless there are other RP reasons why it should be more).

5. Crime & Pursuit Rules

  • 5.0 Vehicles and Decamps:
    • 5.0.1 Criminal Car Swap ‚Äì Cannot decamp into OR take out another vehicle for 10 minutes from the start of a pursuit.
    • 5.0.2 Criminal Vehicle Limit ‚Äì Maximum of 2 additional vehicles for decamp.
    • 5.0.3 Vehicle Selection ‚Äì Criminals must start any crime with a 4-wheel vehicle. Bikes (including quad bikes) can only be used as secondary decamp vehicles.
    • 5.0.4 Seat Limit ‚Äì During crimes, you may not carry/escort people to breach the seat limit in a vehicle. 4 seats means 4 people can be in the car.
  • 5.1 Robberies (and other crimes):
    • 5.1.1 20-minute cooldown between robberies (e.g., if you rob a house, you cannot rob another house for 20 mins, same goes with store robberies, etc.).
    • 5.1.2 10-minute cooldown after ‚Äúthe scene has ended‚Äù (e.g., you have escaped from a pursuit and have not interacted with PD surrounding that scene for 10 mins minimum) before swapping clothes, storing vehicles, or storing proceeds of crimes.
  • 5.2 Green Zones & Players: BLHS Buildings, Courthouse Buildings are safe/green zones.
    • 5.2.1 Cannot use green zones to escape RP.
    • 5.2.2 BLHS employees and PCSO‚Äôs are ‚ÄúGreen Players‚Äù.
    • 5.2.3 BLHS employees can only be hostages with management approval.

    • 5.3.3 You are not allowed to ‚Äúpocket wipe‚Äù a player, they must have food/water, etc.

6. Gang Rules

  • 6.1 Robbery Crew Size: Max 5 players in robberies.
  • 6.2 Hostage Demands: One demand per hostage.
  • 6.3 Whitelisted Gangs: To start a whitelist gang, you must apply via Discord with 3 active members. You cannot be a member of a whitelisted gang whilst also having a PD Soul.
  • 6.4 Gang on Gang: Must have a valid RP reason.
  • 6.5 Value Life: Gang members must still value life/freedom.
  • 6.6 Gang Size: Max 7 gang members + 3 associates.
  • 6.7 Out of City Conduct: No out-of-city toxicity regarding gang conflicts.

7. Whitelisted Job Rules

  • 7.1 Police Numbers at Scenes:
    • 7.1.1 PD must bring a reasonable and proportionate number of officers to a scene.
    • 7.1.2 For robberies: Maximum of 10 officers may directly engage (including negotiator, perimeter, and chase units).
    • 7.1.3 Additional officers may monitor city-wide, but not interfere.
  • 7.2 Vehicle Limits:
    • 7.2.1 At robberies, max 6 pursuit vehicles may actively chase at one time.
    • 7.2.2 Only 2 specialist vehicles (e.g., helicopter) may be deployed.
    • 7.2.3 Helicopter use must be justified IC and approved by an on-duty command officer.
  • 7.3 Vehicle Swapping: PD cannot swap vehicles mid-pursuit, vehicles must be towed/repaired.
  • 7.4 Corruption: No whitelisted job holder may be corrupt without prior authorisation from staff. No PD employee may have a Gang Soul (criminal souls are fine, just not whitelisted gang members).
